Search the web the way you think.

CtrlQuery is a Chrome extension that brings AI-powered semantic search to any webpage. Ask in plain language — find the passages that actually mean what you asked.

Free • Runs locally by default • No account required

Built for how people actually read the web.

Two modes. One shortcut.

SMART SEARCH

Find meaning, not just matches.

Ask a question or describe what you're looking for. CtrlQuery embeds the page locally and returns the passages that actually answer you — even if they don't contain your exact words.

KEYWORD SEARCH

Ctrl+F, but better.

Case-sensitive, whole-word, OR clauses, and Deep Search that probes hidden content behind accordions and dropdowns. All with color-coded per-term highlights.

Works on PDFs

Built-in PDF viewer with the same smart + keyword search.

Local by default

On-device embeddings via transformers.js. Nothing leaves your machine unless you opt in to the OpenAI route.

Color themes

Seven themes. Adaptive toolbar icon that matches your browser's light/dark mode.

Your data stays on your machine.

CtrlQuery only reads the page you search on, and only when you ask. Search indexes live in your browser's local storage. We don't collect, transmit, or sell your browsing data.
