Two modes. One shortcut.

SMART SEARCH

Find meaning, not just matches.

Ask a question or describe what you're looking for. ctrlQuery searches the page and returns the passages that actually match your meaning, not just your exact wording.

KEYWORD SEARCH

Ctrl+F, but better.

Case-sensitive, whole-word, OR clauses, and Deep Search that finds hidden text behind accordions and dropdowns. Plus slash commands like /date, /email, and /number that highlight every match on the page in one tap.
